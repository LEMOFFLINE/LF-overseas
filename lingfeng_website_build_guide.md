### 标题

```text
From Requirement to Finished Garment
```

### 副标题

```text
Every project follows a structured process from requirement confirmation and material preparation to production, inspection and packaging.
```

### 流程节点

```text
01 Requirement Review
Confirm product type, quantity, functional needs, size chart and delivery expectations.

02 Material Selection
Review fabric, color, trims, lining, logo method and performance requirements.

03 Pattern & Sample Development
Support pattern making, sample development and adjustment before bulk production.

04 Fabric Inspection & Cutting
Inspect incoming materials, prepare fabric, make CAD layout and complete cutting.

05 Sewing & Assembly
Proceed with sewing production, garment assembly and key process control.

06 Pressing & Finishing
Handle pressing, shaping, finishing and appearance checking.

07 Final Inspection
Check finished garments, key measurements, workmanship and packaging readiness.

08 Packaging Preparation
Prepare folding, packing, labeling and delivery support based on project requirements.
```

资料里的生产流程包括验布、预缩、样板设计制作、CAD 排版、铺布、裁剪、粘合、缝制、中检、整烫、成检、包装入库，适合重新设计成这个英文流程。

# 13. 给 Codex 的实施说明

可以直接把下面这段给 Codex：

```text
Build a modern B2B website for Beijing Lingfeng Apparel, a China-based custom workwear, uniform and protective apparel manufacturer.

Use a dark navy industrial B2B visual style inspired by international logistics/manufacturing websites: large hero image, dark blue overlay, floating product capability cards, clean white sections, strong statistics, process timeline, factory capability grid, quality control section, RFQ CTA, and a deep navy footer.

Do not create a separate certifications or qualifications page. The website should build trust through production capacity, equipment, manufacturing process, quality control, and project-based customization.

Main pages:
Home
Products
Manufacturing
Quality
About
Contact / RFQ

Design tone:
Industrial, reliable, clean, corporate, international B2B.

Primary colors:
Dark navy, white, light gray, bright blue accent.

Use real factory/manufacturing imagery where possible: workshop, cutting table, sewing line, garment racks, fabric inspection, pressing, packaging.

Avoid fashion-brand style, e-commerce product grid style, excessive certificate display, luxury styling, and fake-looking model images.
```

# 14. 最终页面排版优先级

第一版开发时，优先级如下：

```text
1. 首页必须完整
2. Contact / RFQ 必须可用
3. Products 页面做分类能力，不做 SKU
4. Manufacturing 页面展示流程和设备
5. Quality 页面展示质控逻辑
6. About 页面简洁可信
```

最关键的判断是：

> 网站第一眼要像“真实工厂”，第二眼要像“能接海外询盘”，第三眼要让客户知道“我该怎么把需求发给你”。

这版就按这个方向做。
