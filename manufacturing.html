Overview

Structured Production for Project-Based Apparel

Our workwear and uniform orders are produced through long-term manufacturing resources in Hebei, China. The production site supports pattern making, fabric inspection, cutting, sewing, ironing, inspection and packing for custom garment projects.

Our team coordinates each order from sample development to bulk production and final inspection, helping overseas buyers manage quality, delivery and customization requirements.

Process timeline

From Technical Review to Packaging

Each project moves through clear checkpoints before bulk production and final delivery preparation.

Requirement Review

Review product type, quantity, size chart, functional needs and delivery expectations.

Material Selection

Confirm fabric, trims, color, lining, logo method and performance needs.

Pattern Making

Prepare patterns according to garment structure and client requirements.

Sample Development

Develop and adjust samples before moving toward bulk production.

Fabric Inspection

Check incoming fabric appearance and preparation status.

CAD Layout & Cutting

Plan layout, spread fabric and complete cutting preparation.

Sewing Production

Arrange sewing, assembly and production-stage process checks.

Final Inspection & Packaging

Inspect finished garments, complete pressing, folding, packing and labeling support.
