Quality control

Quality Control

We focus on process-based quality control throughout material preparation, cutting, sewing, finishing, inspection and packaging.

Quality philosophy

Clear Requirements, Controlled Process, Careful Inspection

For customized apparel projects, stable quality depends on clear requirements, suitable materials, coordinated production processes and careful final inspection.

Our team follows production-stage checks with long-term manufacturing partners to reduce defects, improve consistency and support reliable delivery.

Three-stage control

Quality Checks Across Production

Quality control starts before cutting and continues through sewing, finishing and packaging review.

Material Stage

  • Fabric appearance inspection
  • Material preparation
  • Pre-shrinking support
  • Color and fabric confirmation

Production Stage

  • Cutting piece inspection
  • Sewing process self-check
  • Key process inspection
  • In-process quality follow-up

Final Stage

  • Pressing and finishing check
  • Finished garment inspection
  • Needle inspection
  • Packaging review

Supporting quality and management documents can be provided for qualified project discussions upon request.
