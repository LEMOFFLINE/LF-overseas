About LF Clothing and Beijing Lingfeng Apparel

LF Clothing is the overseas inquiry website of Beijing Lingfeng Apparel, a China-based workwear and uniform supplier serving overseas B2B buyers.

Company introduction

Export Service, Project Coordination and Production Resources

LF Clothing represents the overseas inquiry website of Beijing Lingfeng Apparel Co., Ltd., a China-based workwear and uniform supplier serving overseas B2B buyers. We focus on custom workwear, industrial uniforms, work jackets, winter workwear, service uniforms and project-based apparel solutions.

Our Beijing team handles customer communication, product development coordination, quotation, export documentation and order management. Garment production is arranged through our long-term Hebei-based production resources, supported by experienced pattern makers, sewing workers, equipment and quality control procedures.

This structure allows us to combine flexible export service, project coordination and stable garment production capacity for international workwear and uniform orders.
